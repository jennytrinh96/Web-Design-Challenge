Vs. Wind Speed



In this data plot of Latitude vs. Wind Speed, as one travels north of 40 degrees latitude it is expected to experience high wind speed of 25+ mph. These outlying cities with high wind speed may be located in northern areas such as Europe, Russia, or Canada. There are a greater amount of cities north of the equator that experience an average of 5 mph wind speed at this time of year compared to a lesser number of cities south of the equator.
